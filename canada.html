CN Tower

  • Location: Toronto, CN
  • Fun Facts:
    • Lightning strikes the tower around 75 times a year
    • On a clear day, visitors can see over 100mi from the observation deck
    • It was built to withstand an 8.5 magnitude earthquake on the Richter Scale

Olympic Tower Montreal

  • Location: Montreal, QC
  • Fun Facts:
    • It is the highest inclined tower in the world at 550 feet tall
    • It stands at a 45 degree angle
    • The funicular (which is basically an elevator) is the only one that works on a curved structure

Niagara Falls

  • Location: Niagara Falls, CN
  • Fun Facts:
    • It is made up of 3 waterfalls: the American Falls, the Bridal Veil Falls, and the Canadian Falls
      • They combine to make the highest flow rate of any waterfall on Earth
    • Scientists believe that the Niagara Falls will be gone in around 50,000 years at the rate it is eroding at
    • It was created by glacier activity around 10,000 years ago
